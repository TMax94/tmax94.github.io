about

The Company

ICON - small symbolic images is a company founded by the ICON-Group that found it's goal in providing icons and to design those meaningful, rather small graphics. The intended use is the revaluation of user interfaces in the software development to improve the user experience. Additionally we found all kinds of usages for icons in the advertising development. Today we provide a large scale of the icons demanded by our customers and offer them on different buisness websites.

We founded the ICON company to set a new standart in the icon design and provide state of the art icons over upcoming years. This mission includes the provision of a separate platform for the exchange of icons and to give the user a possibility to express their own design suggestions and criticisms.
